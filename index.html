Spotlight Awards 2021

Launched in 2017 for WKWSCI's 25th Anniversary Celebrations, the Alumni Awards has recognised the excellent work of numerous bright and creative alumni. The second edition of Spotlight Awards is a proud culmination of the Alumni Awards and new student award categories.

The Spotlight Awards recognises and celebrates the outstanding work, creativity and spirit of excellence from our students and alumni in the dynamic field of communication and information.

The Spotlight Awards 2021 boasts four main categories, including the Special Recognition and Vanguard brackets. It will be streamed via Facebook Live on 14 August!

The Team

The Spotlight Awards 2021 is supported by WKWSCI and organised by a team of diverse students. The team has worked tirelessly on the Awards for months and is proud to be a part of this initiative to appreciate the talent in WKWSCI.
